Where to go in Cox’s Bazar

  1. Kolatoli Point-

    All the buses of Cox’s Bazar enter through this point. From here you will reach Kolatoli Beach. At this beach, you can rent a tube and go down into the sea. In that case, the tube fare is Tk 40 per hour. You can also go to the sea by water boat/speed boat. The cost will be 300–400 taka (two rounds). You can also drink coconut water to relieve fatigue. The price is 40–50 taka.

  2. Sugandha Point-

    This is the most popular point, so So everyone crowds here. Here is the popular Burmese market. You can enjoy a bike ride on the beach if you want. The cost will be 200 takas-1.5 km and 300 takas-2.5 km. You can also go for a ride on the beach on horseback, the cost may be 50–80 taka (depending on the timing).

  3. Laboni Point-

    Labani Beach is quieter than the other two beaches. If you want to spend your time alone, you can walk here. After an 8–10 minute walk along Sugandha Beach, you will reach this beach. But it is better not to stay here after the evening.

Coxs Bazar Trip’s Cautions and Tips-

  • Starting from the hotel, food, shopping, and vehicle rental, you will bargain everywhere.
  • In Cox’s Bazar, many photographers will want to take pictures. Discuss the quantity and price of the picture before taking the picture. Normally the price of each picture is 3–5 taka.
  • Avoid parasailing if you have a weak heart.
  • If you want to come to Cox’s Bazar at a low cost, you can come in the off-season.
  • Avoid polluting the environment by littering.
